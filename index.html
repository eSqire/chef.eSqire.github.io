Chef Stella, Diner and Catering.


In 2016 we began a journey to serve the southern Alberta city of Lethbridge with passion.since the very day we opened it has been a dream come true .

We are a family oriented restaurant that loves to share our love for the city through our objectively focused dishes that are sure to please any pallate.

So, come on by. Enjoy our Freshly made soups, stews and sauces, or even take a bite out of a signature sandwhich or burger!

Stay a while, or make it a quick trip and grab something fresh out of our homemade market!

We would be ecstatic to greet and/or save a seat for you and your whole family.

Sincerely, Chef Stella, Sarah and the whole family.
